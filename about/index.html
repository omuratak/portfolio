About Me

My name is Takahiro, and I am passionate about improving the lives of others through design, while also making a positive social impact. I am currently a UI/UX design student at the Tokyo Designplex Institute .

Previously, during 2017-2021, I worked as a management consultant for ZS Associates in Tokyo, supporting pharmaceutical companies with sales/marketing analytics and technology. At ZS, I also co-founded the pro bono consulting team to work with local NPOs.

I graduated from Grinnell College , a small liberal arts college, with a BA in Psychology. At Grinnell, I did research on behavioral neuroscience.
